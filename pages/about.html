About Take 1

Take 1 is a website that recommends you specific aid, specially catered to your situation and mood. By taking our short quiz, we can suggest you the resources that are best suited to the problems you are having. In addition to this, we have divided our digital platform into sections depending on your specific origin of stress. We aim to make our website a safe place that is both easily accessed and extremely effective. Mental health problems are the one of the main causes of the burden of disease worldwide. In the UK, it is 28% of the total burden, but only 5.5% of the total UK health research spending is used on the cause.
